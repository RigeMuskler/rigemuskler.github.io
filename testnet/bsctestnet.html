Get to know us more

1. What is the purpose of this platform?

Currently this platform serves as a means for show off or flexxing that crypto muscle. In the crypto world, holders need a safe and fun way to show off their digital assets. What's the point of being crypto-informed; crypto rich and can't show it off?

2. What is the profile holder?

There is only one profile holder at a time. The profile holder is the person who deposits the most Binance Coin (BNB). The profile holder can then update the status. The status is a 300 characters maximum paragraph. This will always be displayed on the profile holder section. The period a person holds as the profile holder decreases the period required to join the 100 Members club. Remember once a person joins the club, the period required is increased. Imagine depositing 100 BNB (refundable) just to post: "Follow me on twitter @cryptoflexclub", now that's a FLEXXX!

3. The Refunds

The platform only charges a fee when you become the profile holder. All other services are free. The fee is indicated in the Club Information Section. All deposits are refunded and can be seen in the My Account Info as pending Refunds. If you are the current profile holder, you must step down first before withdrawing your deposit.

4. What happens when the current profile holder steps down?

The profile holder is refunded. The current Stake required to be a profile holder is reset to the minimum. The status is updated to the default Status. The default status is set by the dev team. This provides a good opportunity for a new profile holder. Remember, your active profile period is updated after you are dethroned or you step down.

5. Are my funds safe?

The funds are secured and protected by the smart contract. The dev team cannot access user deposits but only the fees collected. All deposits are also refunded and can be withdrawn anytime. The deposited funds never leave the smart contract.

6. I am not convinced

To provide more confidence to our users, we used a simple and readable smart contract language. Our smart contract is only 450 lines of code long, well documented and written in python. Anyone with basic programming skills can audit the smart contract and publicly air their opinions. The dev team behind the project is composed of 5 experienced python software engineers.

7. Getting Started

Connect your wallet. MetaMask is recommended for a smooth user experience. Once you connect your MetaMask, the following sections will now be accessible. The profile holder section, my account info section, club info section and tools sections. Your journey begins by becoming the profile holder. Just click update status and enter your own status, the amount in ETH to deposit. Remember, the amount plus network fees will be deducted from your account. Confirm transaction with your wallet and voila! New profile holder in town!

8. What if this website is down?

The smart contract can still be accessed via Etherscan, Remix IDE and even through your local machine, just download the webpage from our github and host it locally. The only thing required is the contract address. The dev team is always ready to help.

9. Is the team anonymous?

Due to current contracts with some of the leading DeFi projects, we cannot reveal our identities yet. Still need that bread! However, if there is need for the team to reveal itself, the club members will be first ones to meet us. The team will not be liable to the content posted on the status. You know people be wilding out in this industry.

10. More about the required profile active period

Joining the 100 members exclusive club is not easy. It requires your profile to be active for a set duration. The required time is shown in the club information section. The active profile period is cumulative, it is added every second your profile is active. The time remaining before you can join the club is indicated in my account info section. Remember, once a member joins the club, the required period is increased. For example, if member number 1 joins the club after 1,000 active profile seconds, the second member might require 2,000 active profile seconds. The active profile duration is cumulative and not a one long continous duration.

11. The Tools?

The platform provides tools to access services offered by the platform. You can update your status, withdraw your pending refunds, step down as the current holder, join the crypto flex club and check whether an account belongs to a club member or not. More tools can be provided upon request by the crypto flex club community.

12. Connect with us

Check the contact section for ways of reaching us. We are on twitter, telegram and email. We are always excited to hear from you.
